Chinese Volunteer Teacher Performances and Teaches Martial Arts At St. George Place IB International School

October 10, 2017

Local time on October 10th, Texas Southern University Confucius Instituteâ€™s Chinese volunteer teacher Heng Zhang went to HISD school district of St. George Place IB International School to teach martial arts course. Wearing traditional martial arts clothing, the teacher gave students a martial arts performance. All the school grades had participated in the martial arts experience activities.

First, teacher Heng Zhang carried out the performance of Tai Chi, which won warm praise from teachers and students. In the martial arts class, students practiced and imitated seriously. In the end, every student were scrambling to show his own moves that he just learned.

The martial arts experience lasted one day, across the school grade, from kindergarten until the fifth grade students, different age students have different characteristics, martial arts teachers took a different teaching content and teaching methods, so that students of different ages can be more happy to feel the fun of martial arts.

At the same time the school teachers were also happy to participate. The school staff and management teachers lifted their phone to took video. The school's leading teachers also praised the martial arts performances held jointly by the Confucius Institute at Southern University and St. George Place IB International School.

Confucius Institute at Southern University has always paid great attention to the cultural cooperation of various Chinese classrooms. It aims to further convey cultural teaching, so that more students can feel the charm and interest of Chinese culture.

  • International Programs Exhibition

August 30, 2016

Texas Southern University (TSU) held a study abroad exhibit in the school museum. TSU offers study abroad programs that travel to various countries such as: Ireland, France, Spain, Tanzania, and China.

The exhibit attracted a large number of TSU students. Students had the opportunity to hear lectures about each study abroad program. Throughout the lectures China came across as the most fascinating for the students per usual.

Alongside the lecture, each study abroad destination had individual information tables inside the museum. Each countries booth style was set up to display the culture of it's native culture. The Chinese Confucius Institute booth was decorated by TSUCI volunteers. As usual, many students were attracted by the Confucius Institute China Summer Trip program, and therefore registered for Chinese language classes.

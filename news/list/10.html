TSUCI 2016 fall enrollment successfully concluded

August 28, 2016

August 8 - August 28, 2016 is the time for the registration of new students at Texas Southern University (TSU). During this period, the Confucius Institute presented Chinese Language Course for Fall in 2016 and the Summer Trip for TSU Students who enrolled Chinese.

Decorated with the Peking Opera mask, Confucius Institute publicity album, the famous Chinese city postcards, Chinese knotting and a series of Chinese characteristics items, TSUCI booth attracted many new students to consult. Volunteers enthusiastically introduced the Confucius Institute for Chinese courses and summer trip programs, students have expressed their great interest.

This year, TSUCI enrolled nearly 40 students in four classes, including two classes of Elementary Chinese, one Elementary Chinese, and one Intermediate Chinese. Until to August 28, the Confucius Institute in the fall of 2016 Chinese classes enrollment reached 37, compared with the number of students in previous years, the fall of 2016 had a huge increase.

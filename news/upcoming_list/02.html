Contemporary Chinese Art exhibition-Chinese Expressions

November 02-13, 2016

Texas Southern University Confucius Institute is holding a contemporary Chinese Art exhibition called Chinese Expressions from 11/02-11/13 at the University Museum.

We would be honored if you could attend our opening ceremony on 11/04 at Texas Southern University Museum from 7-9 pm.

Exhibition Information:

Exhibition Title: Chinese Expression--Contemporary Artists

Openning Ceremony: November 4,2016,7:00pm-9:00pm

Exhibition Period: November 01-13

Address: University Museum,Texas Southern University

FREE PARKING: 3057 Blodgett St. (Parking Garage)

“中国印象——当代艺术家联展”共有王友江、王酩绅、刘泽林、杨越辉、张玉新、宗珉玄、唐慶年、栾良才、徐芸、鞠洪深等中国当代艺术家参展，作品包括绘画、书法等艺术形式。社会的巨变需要心灵的安慰，这就是为什么在中国经济高速发展之际，中国当代艺术也呈现出最蓬勃的发展趋势。凭藉艺术，中国人能在飞速发展的历史潮流中，安身立命，得到些许的平衡；凭藉艺术，世人从中发现东方艺术魅力，增进理解当代中国。祝美术展览圆满成功！

Chinese Expressions: Contemporary Artists brings to the Texas Southern University Museum painting and calligraphy from leading contemporary artists in China. These works express the artists' search for balance and conciliation amidst the rapid economic development and social changes of contemporary China. Through the unique artists sensibilities expressed in these works we can begin to appreciate the search for new forms of beauty in contemporary China.

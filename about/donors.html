United States Congressman Al Green Recognizes TSUCI Donors

April 14, 2013

United States Congressman Al Green presented a “Certificate of Special Congressional Recognition” to the TSU Confucius Institute, a “Resolution from the Congress of the United States of America” declaring April 12, 2013 to be “Confucius Institute at Texas Southern University Day,” and letters of recognition to Houston Asian community donors who contributed to the Confucius Institute. Pictured with the donors and Congressman Green (center), next to him, from left to right, are Chairman Guoyang CAO of BJTU; Deputy Director-General Yongli WANG of Confucius Institute Headquarters; Prof. Docia Rudley; Chinese Consul General Erwen XU; President Rudley; Dean Taylor; and Chairman Ninghong LIU of Jiangxi University of Traditional Chinese Medicine.

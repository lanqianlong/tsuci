TSUCI Successfully Hosts Hanban Book Exhibition

Spetember 09, 2016

At the beginning of the new semester, TSUCI welcomed the Confucius Institute Headquarters (CIHQ) with a book exhibition, which is valued about 40,000 yuan and a total of 964 volumes. After carefully classifying and sorting out by our volunteers and teachers, TSUCI held a Hanban Book Exhibition at TSU from September 7th-9th, 2016, attracting many students to visit.

There are three categories of these Chinese books, Chinese cultural books, reference books, as well as supplementary materials, such as HSK examination counseling books, multimedia learning materials, CD-ROMs about Chinese culture introduction and stationery. Chinese textbooks have all ages from kindergarten to college. The most popular is the Chinese cultural books, from politics to the economy, history to art, travel to food, to various cities in China folk customs.

The book exhibit has achieved the expected results, the students raised their interest in Chinese culture, deepened the understanding of Chinese culture and enhance their passion to continue learning Chinese. After the exhibit, there are more students that read the books at Confucius Institute Office in their spare time.
